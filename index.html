University Campus

The University of Edinburgh's George Square campus is located in the heart of Edinburgh. While on campus, you'll have access to everything you need within short walking distance.

Getting to Campus

The George Square campus is located within walking distance of all student accomodation. The campus is also accessable via a few different forms of public transportation, including university-run shuttles and the city's Lothian busses. For more information, vist the University's transport webpage.
